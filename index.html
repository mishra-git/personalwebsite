Who Am I

My Early Life

My Passion

Despite the lack of formal and balanced education in the refugee camp, I cultivated a strong interest in Information Technology. This field has been my source of hope and survival. Over the years, I have established myself as a Database Engineer, specializing in Oracle, SQL Server, and Sybase technologies.
